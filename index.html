    Our Company

    What does the company do, or offer?

    Our flower shop is a shop where you can choose various sizes and various kinds of flowers according to your preference. The characteristics of our flower shop are not only selling bouquets made of flowers, but also bouquets, flower baskets, and flower boxes consisting of colors, types, and cases that customers want. In addition, customers can purchase not only bouquets but also various plants that can be grown. We aim to provide our customers with the best quality flowers and to inform them how they can grow flowers and plants for an easy long time without withering.

    Potential customers

    Our customers can be a variety of people, including those who buy for special day anniversaries or events, and those who buy to decorate their space, such as houses or shops. Among them, our customers may be those who don't know their tastes well, who are not familiar with flowers, or who are new to growing plants, who can get quite satisfactory products through consultation. Also, people who are difficult to find on the market or want a variety of flowers and plants that are really fresh and diverse can be customers of our flower shop.

    Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

    Our company will catch people's attention using various colors, images and illustrations of flowers, and it will be more popular if we emphasize that flowers and plants are various and in good condition. And the fact that it can be easily purchased through the Internet rather than directly can also attract people's attention.
